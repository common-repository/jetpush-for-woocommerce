Customers shouldn't be strangers. JetPush helps you to know your customers and communicate better

== Description ==

Install JetPush on your WooCommerce site in less than 60 seconds.

Know your customers better and communicate personally to each of them based on who they are and their recent activities in your online store.

= JetPush Features =

* **Universal Customer Profiles** - Track customer interactions from your website, mobile app or in-store orders. Get to know your customers better over time

* **Develop Customer Insight** - See who your customers are and what they are doing instantly as. Understand how and why customers convert

* **Find Similar Customers** - Segment your customers based on who they are and what they are doing right now. No technical knowhow required

* **Connect with Customers** - Get notified when your customers' status changes and communicate with them through personalised targeted messages

* **WOW Your Customers as a Team** - Keep track of all conversations your team had with customers. Set task reminders and never miss another customer follow-up

Discover more of our awesome features at http://www.jetpush.com/

== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =

* WordPress version 3.7 or later
* WooCommerce version 2.1 or later

= Installing JetPush =

1. Add "JetPush for WooCommerce" via the [built-in automatic plugin installer](http://codex.wordpress.org/Managing_Plugins#Automatic_Plugin_Installation). Alternatively, you can extract and copy the plugin folder `woocommerce-jetpush` into the `wp-content/plugins` folder on your site
2. Enable the plugin
3. Go to *WooCommerce* > *Setting* > *Intergration* > *JetPush for WooCommerce*
4. Enter your Jetpush Project API Key and save
5. That's it. You're done!
